Train Anywhere

Nebraska Parkour

We're a community of trained and dedicated tracers in the Omaha and Lincoln area. We train in multiple locations including outdoors and local gyms.

Our passion is to teach others and grow the Nebraska community. We welcome new-comers, anyone of all ages (young and old), professionals, and anyone else who has ever had an interest in parkour.

Get Involved!

Want to get learn parkour, but have no idea where to go? Don't worry, we've got you covered. Along with our weekly training meetups, we provide:

  • Private/Group Lessons
  • Indoor/Outdoor Classes
  • Training Meetups
  • Coaching Opportunities

Get involved with NEPK and learn more about other opportunities!

Contact Us!

Our goal is to build the parkour community. Whatever you're interested in, so are we. Shoot us a message and we'll get back to you soon!
