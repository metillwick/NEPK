Want to meet up?

During the warmer seasons, we hold weekly events according to our schedules and the weather.
Throughout the year, we meet at gymnastics facilities to train inside.

Check out our Facebook page for when we meet up! See more info according to your area!
NEPK is managed in Omaha. Lincoln Parkour is the referred group in that area.

Omaha

The Omaha parkour group trains in the Gene Leahy Mall. They attend different gym locations for inside training.

Members typically meet on Saturday, if not on Sunday. Click here to see when members plan to train on the Facebook Page!

Lincoln

The Lincoln parkour group has created Flatwater Freerunning, and has established their own gym.

Members typically meet on Sunday afternoon in various locations. Click here to see when members plan to train on the Facebook page!

Not seeing anyone near you?

Don't be sad. We feel you. Nebraska can be pretty spread out, but we might have a couple contacts depending on where you live.

If not, we can help get you started by sending you tutorials, videos, safety instructions, and more! Just fill out a Contact Form!
