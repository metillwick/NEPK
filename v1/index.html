Nebraska Parkour

We're a community of trained and dedicated athletes in the Omaha and Lincoln area. We train in multiple locations including outdoors and local gyms.

Get Involved!

Along with our weekly training meetups, we provide:

  • Private/Group Lessons
  • Indoor/Outdoor Classes
  • Training Meetups
  • Job Opportunities to be a Coach

Get involved with NEPK and learn more about other opportunities!

Contact Us!

Our goal is to build the parkour community. Whatever you're interested in, so are we. Shoot us a message and we'll get back to you soon!
