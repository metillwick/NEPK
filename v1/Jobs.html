Join the Movement!

We are always looking for coaches to help improve the lives of our students and teach them to be better human being!
We also teach them parkour. Fill out a Contact Form and we'll give you more details!

Below are some of criteria we're looking for in our coaches.

Athletic Ability

Though we may make exceptions, most of the time you have to demonstrate a move to show students. From the pedagogical standpoint, this helps produce the best results in our athletes.

Self-Improvement and Growth

We're always looking to improve - as an athlete, as a coach, and as a person. We expect you to improve in all aspects. This means taking criticism well and always working for growth.

Knowledge and Responsibility

We'll teach you how to do parkour and how to coach it. However, you need to know the difference between a good call and a bad one, and take responsibility when things go wrong.

Experience (Preferred)

The best coaches are those who feel the movements and have trained consistently enough to know what works and what doesn't. Don't worry though. We've hired people to learn parkour.

Personality and Character

Above all else, we want to build an inclusive culture geared towards good character, ethics, and morals. We're raising our athletes to have the fundamental human values to help others. So should you.
